# Wedding & Events Planning — App Concepts (Session 35)
> 2026-06-24 | Domain: Wedding & Events Planning

## Concept 1: 囍 (Xi) — Bicultural Wedding Planner
**Subdomain**: xi.ljding.app

**One-line pitch**: A bilingual (EN/ZH) wedding planning hub that weaves together Western and Chinese wedding traditions in one timeline.

**Core functionality**:
- Unified timeline merging Western 12-month plan with Chinese tradition checkpoints (betrothal, tea ceremony, etc.)
- Guest list with RSVP, dietary needs, plus-ones, plus automatic RMB/USD budget tracking
- Bilingual invitation builder with Chinese character art, tea ceremony cards, and Western suite options
- Vendor tracker with bilingual contract templates
- 红包 (red envelope) guidance tracker — family contributions, amounts, reciprocity tracking

**Primary user**: Chinese-American couples planning bicultural weddings, diaspora couples

**Key differentiator**: Only web-first bilingual wedding planner. All competitors (The Knot, Zola, WeddingWire) are English-only or China-only mobile apps. Genuine white space.

**Monetization**: Free tier with 100 guests/10 vendors; $15/mo unlimited

## Concept 2: 赴宴 (FuYan) — Guest Experience Web App
**Subdomain**: fuyan.ljding.app

**One-line pitch**: A web-based guest experience portal — beautiful RSVP, digital guest cards, day-of schedule, and photo sharing.

**Core functionality**:
- Wedding website with RSVP, accommodation info, registry links, day-of schedule
- Digital "guest card" — share photos, well wishes, dietary notes
- Web-based seating chart viewer (no app download needed)
- Post-wedding photo gallery with guest uploads
- QR code table assignments for walk-in events

**Primary user**: Wedding guests of all ages (web-first for accessibility), couples wanting elegant guest experience

**Key differentiator**: Most wedding websites are mobile-first; FuYan is explicitly web-first and focuses on guest experience rather than couple planning. Desktop view for ceremony planning on larger screens.

**Monetization**: $10/event basic; $30/event premium with custom domain + analytics

## Concept 3: 缘定 (YuanDing) — Destination Wedding Hub
**Subdomain**: yuanding.ljding.app

**One-line pitch**: A web app for couples planning destination weddings, combining venue discovery, guest travel coordination, and local vendor networks.

**Core functionality**:
- Venue discovery for popular destination wedding locations (Bali, Tuscany,三亚, Phuket)
- Group travel coordination — bulk room booking, flight price alerts
- Local vendor marketplace for destination locations
- Guest gifting suite (welcome bags, farewell brunch)
- Timeline for destination weekend (welcome dinner → ceremony → farewell brunch)
- Weather contingency planning tools

**Primary user**: Couples planning destination weddings, particularly diaspora couples returning to Asia

**Key differentiator**: No competitor combines venue discovery + travel coordination + local vendor marketplace. Most tools are either venue OR travel, not both.

**Monetization**: Venue commission 5%, premium venue featured placement, $20/mo for vendor tools

## Concept 4: 红封 (HongFeng) — Red Envelope Culture Guide & Tracker
**Subdomain**: hongfeng.ljding.app

**One-line pitch**: A bilingual guide to 红包 (red envelope) etiquette + tracker for managing family contributions and reciprocity.

**Core functionality**:
- Bilingual EN/ZH guide to red envelope customs (birthday, wedding, Chinese New Year, etc.)
- Amount calculator based on relationship, occasion, region
- Tracker for who gave you 红包, amounts, future reciprocity obligations
- Family contribution tracker (谁给了多少, 谁应该给什么)
- Integration with wedding budget for tracking family contributions
- Reminders for reciprocity occasions

**Primary user**: Chinese diaspora individuals navigating family financial expectations, especially around weddings

**Key differentiator**: No standalone web app for red envelope culture. This is a genuinely novel tool for diaspora Chinese managing complex family financial relationships.

**Monetization**: Free with ads; $5/mo for tracker + no ads; premium includes custom amounts database

## Concept 5: 礼单 (LiDan) — Unified Wedding Registry
**Subdomain**: lidan.ljding.app

**One-line pitch**: A privacy-first, unified wedding registry that aggregates gifts from multiple registries into one elegant guest experience.

**Core functionality**:
- Connect existing registries (Amazon, Zola, Target) into one dashboard
- "Cash fund" options with configurable allocation (honeymoon, house fund, charity)
- Gift tracking — who gave what, thank-you note reminders
- Bilingual gift tracking for Chinese family members
- Privacy controls — hide certain items from certain guests
- Post-wedding thank-you note tracker

**Primary user**: Couples overwhelmed by fragmented registries, guests wanting one-stop gift shopping

**Key differentiator**: Privacy-first (no account required for guests to view), bilingual toggle, unified view. Zola locks guests into their ecosystem; LiDan is registry-agnostic.

**Monetization**: 1% gift processing fee; $10/mo for premium analytics + thank-you automation
