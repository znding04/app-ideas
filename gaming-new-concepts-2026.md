## Key Insights

1. **Daily engagement > async multiplayer for MVP**: ChengYu (daily puzzle) is easier to ship and has better retention than complex multiplayer games.

2. **Cultural content is the moat**: MahJam's Chinese regional rules, ChengYu's idiom collection, Heritage Quiz's question bank — these require cultural expertise that's hard to copy.

3. **Web-first is the differentiator**: All competitors are mobile-only or have dated web experiences. Clean web UX with PWA offline support is genuinely novel.

4. **Family sync over random matchmaking**: Diaspora families play together across time zones. Persistent family rooms with async take-a-turn mechanics outperform real-time matchmaking.

5. **Bilingual is non-negotiable**: Mixed-fluency families (English-speaking kids, Chinese-speaking grandparents) need EN/ZH toggle on everything.
