## Scoring Rationale (Mindfulness Apps)

### 静心 (JingXin) (18/20) — Recommended #1 Mindfulness App
- **Feasibility 4/5**: Guided audio playback, timer engine, mood tracking, PWA offline. ~5-7 weeks for full MVP.
- **Differentiation 5/5**: No web-first bilingual (EN/ZH) meditation app exists. Calm/Headspace are English-only mobile; Chinese apps lack web. Clear white space.
- **Monetization 4/5**: $7/mo for offline downloads + premium analytics; free tier covers core use. Reasonable conversion from free.
- **Domain Fit 5/5**: Strong — connects sleep.ljding.app (evening wind-down), learn.ljding.app (pre-study focus), and mood data from xinqing.ljding.app. Privacy-first aligns with whole ecosystem philosophy.

### 心情簿 (XinQingBu) (17/20)
- **Feasibility 5/5**: 100% local storage, no backend needed for core MVP. 2-3 week build time.
- **Differentiation 4/5**: Privacy-first mood journal is differentiated from mood tracking apps that require accounts. Rule-based recommendations are unique.
- **Monetization 3/5**: Limited monetization without cloud sync. Could offer "cloud backup + cross-device" as premium.
- **Domain Fit 5/5**: Serves as data source for other apps (Radar burnout, Drift focus quality, JingXin recommendations). High integration value.

### 吐纳 (TuNa) (17/20)
- **Feasibility 5/5**: Visual breath guide with SVG animation; no audio needed. 3-4 weeks.
- **Differentiation 4/5**: Dedicated breathwork app with visual-first design. No web-first competitor in this space.
- **Monetization 4/5**: $5/mo for HRV integration + advanced patterns. Breathwork users are engaged and willing to pay for optimization.
- **Domain Fit 4/5**: Complements JingXin (breathe → meditate journey). Works well during work breaks on desktop.

### 同修 (TongXiu) (17/20)
- **Feasibility 3/5**: Real-time audio sync is complex. "Start at same time" approach simplifies but loses the core differentiator.
- **Differentiation 5/5**: Group meditation with synchronized ambient audio is genuinely novel. No web-first competitor.
- **Monetization 4/5**: $10/mo for unlimited sessions + recording/sharing. Premium justified for the social experience.
- **Domain Fit 5/5**: Strongest hangwith.ljding.app integration of all concepts. Group meditation is a natural social feature.

### 禅修 (ChanXiu) (15/20)
- **Feasibility 3/5**: Multi-tradition timer is straightforward; content sourcing (bilingual sutras, gathas) is the challenge.
- **Differentiation 5/5**: Buddhist contemplation + text study + multi-tradition support. No competitor combines these.
- **Monetization 3/5**: Niche audience limits scale. Could partner with Buddhist organizations for content licensing.
- **Domain Fit 4/5**: Fits well for serious practitioners. Could integrate with learn.ljding.app for religious/philosophical study tracks.

## Scoring Rationale (Health Apps)

### Radar (17/20) — Recommended #1 Wellness App
- **Feasibility 5/5**: Weekly check-in form + traffic-light dashboard + 5 indicators. No wearables, no complex integrations. MVP in 2-3 weeks.
- **Differentiation 4/5**: No consumer app tracks burnout *leading indicators*. Clinical platforms (Spring Health, Lyra) don't serve individuals. Clear gap fill.
- **Monetization 4/5**: Clear free/pro split. $6/mo for trend data and export is reasonable for freelancers/students.
- **Domain Fit 4/5**: Complements learn (sustainable learning requires managing burnout risk) and hangwith (energy for social connection).

### Stack (16/20)
- **Feasibility 4/5**: Behavior science logic is complex for v1. Needs goal selection + habit generation + tracking + outcome metrics. 4-6 weeks for full.
- **Differentiation 4/5**: The "anti-streak" approach (downgrade instead of punishing) is genuinely different. Habit stacking is well-researched but poorly implemented.
- **Monetization 4/5**: $7/mo for AI adaptation + outcome reports is justified by the behavior change value prop.
- **Domain Fit 4/5**: Natural fit — health habits support learning focus and social energy.

### StressMap (15/20)
- **Feasibility 3/5**: Requires wearable API integrations (Apple Watch, Whoop, Oura) + calendar integration + HRV analysis. 6-8 weeks minimum.
- **Differentiation 5/5**: No app currently connects "your HRV dropped" to "because you had 3 back-to-back meetings after 5 hours of sleep." Completely novel.
- **Monetization 4/5**: $8/mo for wearable + calendar sync + forecasts. Whoop users already pay $30/mo so this is a reasonable secondary tool.
- **Domain Fit 3/5**: Loosely related — stress patterns affect learning and social, but the app itself doesn't connect to those domains.
