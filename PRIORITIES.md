## Scoring Rationale (Mindfulness Apps)

### 静心 (JingXin) (18/20) — Recommended #1 Mindfulness App
- **Feasibility 4/5**: Guided audio playback, timer engine, mood tracking, PWA offline. ~5-7 weeks for full MVP.
- **Differentiation 5/5**: No web-first bilingual (EN/ZH) meditation app exists. Calm/Headspace are English-only mobile; Chinese apps lack web. Clear white space.
- **Monetization 4/5**: $7/mo for offline downloads + premium analytics; free tier covers core use. Reasonable conversion from free.
- **Domain Fit 5/5**: Strong — connects sleep.ljding.app (evening wind-down), learn.ljding.app (pre-study focus), and mood data from xinqing.ljding.app. Privacy-first aligns with whole ecosystem philosophy.

### 心情簿 (XinQingBu) (17/20)
- **Feasibility 5/5**: 100% local storage, no backend needed for core MVP. 2-3 week build time.
- **Differentiation 4/5**: Privacy-first mood journal is differentiated from mood tracking apps that require accounts. Rule-based recommendations are unique.
- **Monetization 3/5**: Limited monetization without cloud sync. Could offer "cloud backup + cross-device" as premium.
- **Domain Fit 5/5**: Serves as data source for other apps (Radar burnout, Drift focus quality, JingXin recommendations). High integration value.

### 吐纳 (TuNa) (17/20)
- **Feasibility 5/5**: Visual breath guide with SVG animation; no audio needed. 3-4 weeks.
- **Differentiation 4/5**: Dedicated breathwork app with visual-first design. No web-first competitor in this space.
- **Monetization 4/5**: $5/mo for HRV integration + advanced patterns. Breathwork users are engaged and willing to pay for optimization.
- **Domain Fit 4/5**: Complements JingXin (breathe → meditate journey). Works well during work breaks on desktop.

### 同修 (TongXiu) (17/20)
- **Feasibility 3/5**: Real-time audio sync is complex. "Start at same time" approach simplifies but loses the core differentiator.
- **Differentiation 5/5**: Group meditation with synchronized ambient audio is genuinely novel. No web-first competitor.
- **Monetization 4/5**: $10/mo for unlimited sessions + recording/sharing. Premium justified for the social experience.
- **Domain Fit 5/5**: Strongest hangwith.ljding.app integration of all concepts. Group meditation is a natural social feature.

### 禅修 (ChanXiu) (15/20)
- **Feasibility 3/5**: Multi-tradition timer is straightforward; content sourcing (bilingual sutras, gathas) is the challenge.
- **Differentiation 5/5**: Buddhist contemplation + text study + multi-tradition support. No competitor combines these.
- **Monetization 3/5**: Niche audience limits scale. Could partner with Buddhist organizations for content licensing.
- **Domain Fit 4/5**: Fits well for serious practitioners. Could integrate with learn.ljding.app for religious/philosophical study tracks.

## Scoring Rationale (Health Apps)

### Radar (17/20) — Recommended #1 Wellness App
- **Feasibility 5/5**: Weekly check-in form + traffic-light dashboard + 5 indicators. No wearables, no complex integrations. MVP in 2-3 weeks.
- **Differentiation 4/5**: No consumer app tracks burnout *leading indicators*. Clinical platforms (Spring Health, Lyra) don't serve individuals. Clear gap fill.
- **Monetization 4/5**: Clear free/pro split. $6/mo for trend data and export is reasonable for freelancers/students.
- **Domain Fit 4/5**: Complements learn (sustainable learning requires managing burnout risk) and hangwith (energy for social connection).

### Stack (16/20)
- **Feasibility 4/5**: Behavior science logic is complex for v1. Needs goal selection + habit generation + tracking + outcome metrics. 4-6 weeks for full.
- **Differentiation 4/5**: The "anti-streak" approach (downgrade instead of punishing) is genuinely different. Habit stacking is well-researched but poorly implemented.
- **Monetization 4/5**: $7/mo for AI adaptation + outcome reports is justified by the behavior change value prop.
- **Domain Fit 4/5**: Natural fit — health habits support learning focus and social energy.

### StressMap (15/20)
- **Feasibility 3/5**: Requires wearable API integrations (Apple Watch, Whoop, Oura) + calendar integration + HRV analysis. 6-8 weeks minimum.
- **Differentiation 5/5**: No app currently connects "your HRV dropped" to "because you had 3 back-to-back meetings after 5 hours of sleep." Completely novel.
- **Monetization 4/5**: $8/mo for wearable + calendar sync + forecasts. Whoop users already pay $30/mo so this is a reasonable secondary tool.
- **Domain Fit 3/5**: Loosely related — stress patterns affect learning and social, but the app itself doesn't connect to those domains.

## Scoring Rationale (Education Apps)

### Recall (18/20) — Recommended #1 Education App
- **Feasibility 5/5**: Card CRUD + FSRS algorithm + clean review UI + local-first storage. 4-6 weeks MVP. No novel ML needed.
- **Differentiation 4/5**: Web-native SRS with beautiful UX is clear gap vs Anki (desktop-only, ugly). Bilingual EN/ZH support is bonus differentiation.
- **Monetization 4/5**: $6/mo for cloud sync + cross-device + analytics. Free tier covers core SRS use. Reasonable conversion path.
- **Domain Fit 5/5**: Core to learn.ljding.app — serves as the study engine for all course content. Also feeds into Drift (focus quality), Radar (study load burnout).

### Passage (18/20)
- **Feasibility 4/5**: URL save + Mercury extraction + clean reader + LLM card generation. 4-5 weeks MVP. Article extraction is the tricky part.
- **Differentiation 5/5**: No tool combines save → read → recall in one web-native flow. Pocket doesn't do recall; Anki doesn't do reading. Clear gap.
- **Monetization 4/5**: $5/mo for unlimited saves + AI card gen + cloud sync. Readers are engaged and value retention.
- **Domain Fit 5/5**: Strong with learn (reading as learning), recall (SRS pipeline), review (article review with spaced repetition).

### Cohort (17/20)
- **Feasibility 5/5**: Group CRUD + check-ins + simple calendar + stats. 2-3 weeks MVP. Simplest app in the education domain.
- **Differentiation 4/5**: Informal study group tracking is novel — most tools are for formal courses (Luma, Maven). This serves the casual learner.
- **Monetization 3/5**: $5/mo for unlimited groups + shared sessions + analytics. Could be limited by group dynamics (one pays for the group).
- **Domain Fit 5/5**: Strongest hangwith.ljding.app integration. Group study accountability is a natural social feature extension.

### Explain (16/20)
- **Feasibility 4/5**: LLM API + adaptive difficulty loop + practice problem generation + simple UI. 4-5 weeks MVP.
- **Differentiation 4/5**: Adaptive explanations at your level is clear angle. Quizlet/Similar don't do depth-adaptive explanations.
- **Monetization 4/5**: $7/mo for unlimited sessions + advanced problem types + progress reports. Free tier with daily limit.
- **Domain Fit 4/5**: Deep learn.ljding.app integration as the AI tutor layer. Works as standalone too.

### Translate (16/20)
- **Feasibility 3/5**: DeepL translation + vocabulary extraction + SRS + CJK rendering. 6-8 weeks MVP. CJK handling is non-trivial.
- **Differentiation 5/5**: Bilingual reading + inline translation + vocabulary tracking + SRS is completely novel. No tool does all three.
- **Monetization 4/5**: $7/mo for unlimited vocabulary + advanced SRS + progress analytics. Vocabulary learners are highly motivated payers.
- **Domain Fit 4/5**: Fits with learn.ljding.app (language learning vertical) and recall.ljding.app (vocab SRS integration).

## Scoring Rationale (Finance Apps)

### TaxTwin (18/20) — Recommended #1 Finance App
- **Feasibility 3/5**: Dual-country tax rules are complex; simplified MVP covers W-2 + self-employment for US and China. 5-6 weeks.
- **Differentiation 5/5**: No tool addresses US+China dual tax compliance. Genuine white space with high-stakes need.
- **Monetization 5/5**: $10/mo for expert mode (dual filing calculators, document checklists). High-income diaspora users are price-insensitive for tax compliance.
- **Domain Fit 5/5**: Direct learn.ljding.app integration for tax education modules. Appeals to high-value users with complex cross-border income.

### GoalSprint (17/20)
- **Feasibility 5/5**: Pure goal tracking + pledge mechanic + streaks. 3 weeks MVP. No novel ML needed.
- **Differentiation 4/5**: Loss-aversion pledge mechanic (miss goal = donate) is genuinely novel. YNAB/Monarch have goals but no pledge system.
- **Monetization 4/5**: Freemium (3 goals free, 10 goals/$5/mo). High engagement = natural premium upgrade path.
- **Domain Fit 4/5**: Strong learn.ljding.app integration (psychology of money content). Gamification proven to drive retention.

### RemiFlow (17/20)
- **Feasibility 4/5**: Rate comparison APIs are public; receipt generation is standard. 4 weeks.
- **Differentiation 5/5**: First web-first bilingual remittance tracker. No competitor combines EN/ZH + rate comparison + receipt logging.
- **Monetization 4/5**: Premium rate alerts + referral fees from Wise/Revolut partnerships. Free tier covers basic use.
- **Domain Fit 4/5**: Family remittance connects learn.ljding.app (financial literacy) + HangWith (family coordination).

### SplitPact (16/20)
- **Feasibility 4/5**: Multi-currency conversion + pact system + reimbursement workflow. 4 weeks.
- **Differentiation 4/5**: Multi-currency couples tracking is novel. HouseFund handles joint accounts; SplitPact handles "I owe you" between partners.
- **Monetization 4/5**: $3/mo per couple (reasonable). Premium for unlimited transaction history.
- **Domain Fit 4/5**: Complements HouseFund (joint vs separate accounts). HangWith integration for couples coordination.

### QuotaGuard (15/20)
- **Feasibility 5/5**: Quota tracking with progress bars + alerts. 3 weeks. Simplest finance MVP.
- **Differentiation 4/5**: SubAware tracks subscriptions; QuotaGuard prevents over-subscription by tracking actual usage vs limits. Browser extension is novel.
- **Monetization 3/5**: Freemium (5 quotas free, 20 quotas/$4/mo). Lower ceiling than others.
- **Domain Fit 3/5**: Pairs with SubAware; expands learn.ljding.app content on hidden costs.
