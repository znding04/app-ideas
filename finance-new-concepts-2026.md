# Finance App Concepts — Session 24 (2026-06-13)

> Research-backed app opportunities for the Finance domain. Focus: personal finance for consumers aged 25-45.

---

## Pain Points Identified

1. **Variable/irregular income underserved** — 15% of families have freelancers/gig workers with tools designed for W-2 salaried workers
2. **Fragmented financial tools** — budgeting, investing, subscriptions, net worth tracked in separate apps, no unified view
3. **Subscription creep goes undetected** — costs multiply silently, apps rarely surface actionable cancellation recommendations
4. **Couples lack shared financial planning** — misaligned spending priorities create conflict without data-driven resolution
5. **Reactive > proactive** — current tools alert after overspending, not guide behavior before mistakes

---

## App Concepts

### SubAudit (subaudit.ljding.app)
- **English Name**: SubAudit — Subscription Value Tracker
- **One-liner**: Auto-detects recurring charges, scores their value based on your usage, and helps you cancel in one tap
- **Complexity**: 2/5 (lowest — MVP needs no bank API, manual or screenshot-based entry)
- **Differentiation**: Goes beyond listing subscriptions — scores each by actual usage frequency and cost-per-use, flags free trials about to convert, provides direct cancellation links
- **Why ljding.app**: Addresses universal pain point (subscription creep), lightweight utility fits alongside learn/hangwith, affiliate monetization potential, fast MVP
- **Monetization**: Affiliate fees from alternative service recommendations, freemium with premium analytics

### FlexBudget (flexbudget.ljding.app)
- **English Name**: FlexBudget — Variable Income Budgeting
- **One-liner**: Adapts monthly spending plans based on actual earnings from freelance, gig, and side-hustle work
- **Complexity**: 3/5
- **Differentiation**: Most budgeters assume fixed biweekly paychecks. FlexBudget dynamically adjusts spending envelopes, savings targets, and bill priorities each month based on actual variable income logged
- **Why ljding.app**: Complements QuantConnect hobby, serves underserved population (gig workers, freelancers)
- **Monetization**: $5/mo premium for multi-income source integration, goal tracking

### PairFi (pairfi.ljding.app)
- **English Name**: PairFi — Couples Finance Alignment
- **One-liner**: Helps partners set shared goals, split expenses fairly, and resolve money disagreements with data
- **Complexity**: 3/5
- **Differentiation**: Existing apps treat finances as individual. Focuses on relationship layer — shared dashboards, goal negotiation, fairness-aware splitting that accounts for income disparity
- **Why ljding.app**: Complements 找谁玩 (hangwith.ljding.app) — after hanging out with friends, couples track finances together
- **Monetization**: $8/mo for couples premium (shared vaults, conflict resolution analytics, multi-account sync)

### NudgeWallet (nudgewallet.ljding.app)
- **English Name**: NudgeWallet — Proactive AI Finance Coach
- **One-liner**: Intervenes before you overspend, not after — contextual nudges based on spending patterns and goals
- **Complexity**: 4/5
- **Differentiation**: Shifts from reactive alerts to predictive nudges. Uses spending velocity, upcoming bills, and goal progress to send timely interventions (e.g., "You're on pace to exceed dining budget by Thursday")
- **Why ljding.app**: AI-powered coach fits the "serious learning" ecosystem of learn.ljding.app
- **Monetization**: $10/mo for AI coaching tier, behavioral insights dashboard

### WealthPulse (wealthpulse.ljding.app)
- **English Name**: WealthPulse — Unified Financial Dashboard
- **One-liner**: Combines spending, investments, debt, and net worth in one simple view with weekly health reports
- **Complexity**: 4/5
- **Differentiation**: Bridges gap between budgeting apps and investment trackers. One screen shows how daily spending decisions affect long-term wealth trajectory. Complements QuantConnect with daily spending context
- **Why ljding.app**: Natural companion to QuantConnect hobby — connects trading activity to daily financial habits
- **Monetization**: $7/mo for investment sync, weekly PDF reports, advanced net worth analytics

---

## Recommended Concept: SubAudit

**Why SubAudit over others:**
- Lowest complexity (2/5) — fast MVP, no bank API integrations needed initially
- Universal pain point affects nearly all 25-45 year olds
- Clear monetization via affiliate model
- Fits as a lightweight utility alongside existing learn + hangwith apps
- High viral/shareability potential ("I saved $X/month")

**MVP Scope**: Manual subscription entry + usage scoring + free trial tracking + cancellation link aggregator. Expand later with bank feed, AI value scoring.
