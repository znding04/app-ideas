### Scoring Criteria
- **Opportunity Score**: Composite of Market Demand (30%), Feasibility (25%), Differentiation (25%), Strategic Fit (20%)
- **Demand**: High = clear market validation, Medium = emerging trend, Low = niche or unproven
- **Feasibility**: High = achievable with current tech, Medium = requires additional resources, Low = complex or untested
- **Differentiation**: Strong = clear unique angle, Moderate = some overlap, Weak = crowded market
